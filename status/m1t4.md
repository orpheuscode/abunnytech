## Delivered

- **Script adaptation** (`media_pipeline.script_adaptation`): splits `VideoBlueprint` hook, outline beats, and CTA into timed `ScriptSegment` rows that sum to `duration_seconds_target`.
- **Captions / metadata** (`media_pipeline.captions`): final caption, normalized hashtags, upload title, alt text from blueprint + `IdentityMatrix` persona disclosure.
- **ElevenLabs-shaped audio request** (`media_pipeline.audio_elevenlabs`): `ElevenLabsAudioRequest` + `MockElevenLabsAdapter` (no network).
- **Nano Banana–shaped video request** (`media_pipeline.video_nano_banana`): prompt/avatar/duration + `MockNanoBananaAdapter`.
- **Post-production manifest** (`media_pipeline.postprod_manifest`): overlays from segments, subtitle track spec, 9:16 crop, optional trending-audio underlay when `blueprint.audio_id` is set.
- **Redo / directives** (`media_pipeline.redo`): merges `RedoQueueItem.payload` and `OptimizationDirectiveEnvelope` when `DirectiveTargetStage.STAGE2` is listed (conventional keys: `new_hook`, `new_title`, `new_caption`, `extra_hashtags`, `replace_outline`, `duration_seconds_target`, `audio_id`).
- **Variants** (`media_pipeline.variants`): additional `ContentPackage` rows for carousel PNGs and story frames (image MIME) alongside the primary short.
- **Orchestration** (`abunny_stage2_generation.pipeline.run_stage2_generation`): used by `pipeline_stage2_generate.service.run_stage2`; persists `content_package` + `stage2_variant_packages` artifacts; writes dry-run JSON under `artifacts/<run_id>/stage2/`.
