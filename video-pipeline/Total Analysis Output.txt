### INTERNAL SYNTHESIS
The chosen concepts are influenced by the recurring success of the "Fall Favorites" haul format (Videos 1, 2, and 4) and the "Dewy Application" style (Videos 3 and 5). These videos consistently use direct eye contact, warm cinematic lighting, and slow product rotations to establish trust and visual desire.

### CONCEPT 1: THE TACTILE GLOW (Creative Variation)
Focuses on the high-detail application and texture seen in the top-performing skincare and makeup clips.

**VEO_PROMPT:**
A woman with radiant skin sits at a marble table. She holds a sleek amber glass bottle and dispenses a clear droplet onto her palm. Close-up on her fingertips blending the liquid into her cheek. Cinematic rim lighting highlights her glowing complexion. The camera performs a slow push-in toward her face as she smiles warmly. Soft, blurred background with warm highlights.

### CONCEPT 2: THE CURATED LIFESTYLE (The Proven Winner)
Replicates the high-performing "curated favorites" format using a desk-bound setup with multiple lifestyle items and high-end lighting.

**VEO_PROMPT:**
A woman sits at a wooden desk under warm, inviting light. She picks up a dark, elegant glass bottle, rotating it slowly to catch the light. She sets it down and puts on thick-rimmed sunglasses, adjusting them with a confident smile. Soft shadows and high-end aesthetic. The camera pans smoothly across the desktop items, ending on her direct eye contact and natural hand gestures.
